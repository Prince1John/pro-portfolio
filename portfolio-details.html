Project Overview

This comprehensive project for Ektron Insurance Company involved creating a cohesive brand presence across multiple platforms. The challenge was to communicate complex insurance concepts in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand manner while maintaining professional credibility.

Project Objectives

  • Develop a unified brand identity for Ektron Insurance
  • Create marketing materials that resonate with target audience
  • Improve client engagement through professional design
  • Establish consistency across all communication channels

Solutions Provided

I designed professional posters and marketing materials that effectively communicate the company's insurance products and services. The design approach focused on:

  • Visual Hierarchy: Clear organization of information for better readability
  • Brand Consistency: Maintaining color schemes and typography across all materials
  • Audience Targeting: Different designs for employee communication vs. client promotion
  • Modern Aesthetics: Contemporary design elements that appeal to both traditional and younger audiences

Results & Impact

  • ✅ 87% increase in client recognition
  • ✅ Successful internal communication campaigns
  • ✅ Professional brand establishment in the insurance sector
  • ✅ Foundation for future marketing initiatives
